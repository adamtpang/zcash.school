zcash.school The thesis in 3 steps

The thesis

Cash was private. Bitcoin gave that up.
Zcash gets it back.

CASH · 1971–2008

Private by default

You handed someone a coin and the transaction left no public record. The original privacy technology for value transfer.

BITCOIN · 2009

Pseudonymous

Anyone with an internet connection can see every transaction. Pseudonyms collapse the moment you cash out or post a donation address.

ZCASH · 2016

Shielded by default

Zero-knowledge proofs prove a transaction is valid without revealing the amount, sender, or recipient. Privacy of cash + auditability of a chain.
