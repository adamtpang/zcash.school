> *We post in the open so anyone can see what the grant funds.*

This is the at-a-glance tracker for the **[Zcash Network School](/ns/)**
grant: a 90-day Zcash Foundation community-grant program, May to July
2026. The detailed deliverable table and the site roadmap live on
**[/roadmap](/roadmap/)**; the narrative monthly reports live on
**[the forum](https://forum.zcashcommunity.com/t/zcash-network-school/55269)**
and mirror to **[/blog](/blog/)**.

<GrantDashboard />

<Aside type="tip" title="How to read this">
Each bar fills to the current count. The vertical tick marks where we
should be **today** given how much of the program window has elapsed.
Bar past the tick: ahead. Bar at the tick: on track. Bar well short of
the tick: behind, and it shows up yellow or red.
</Aside>

## How this updates

The numbers come from one file, `src/data/grant.json`. After each event,
office-hours session, wallet-activation batch, or merchant install, edit
the `current` count and redeploy. Pace recomputes automatically from
today's date against the program window.
