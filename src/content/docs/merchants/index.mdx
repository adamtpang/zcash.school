> *Cash without the surveillance state. Imagine that.*

This page is for **the cafés, food vendors, laundries, barbers, and
online shops at Network School** (and anyone else who wants to take
Zcash). The Zcash Network School grant covers free onboarding — a 15-
minute setup gets you accepting shielded ZEC.

## Why bother

- **Customers at NS will arrive with ZEC.** That's part of the program.
  If you accept it, you'll have customers your competitor won't.
- **No chargebacks.** Crypto payments are final once confirmed. The
  ~1% you used to lose to chargeback fraud stays in your pocket.
- **Near-zero fees.** Card networks take 1.5–3.5%. Zcash fees are
  fractions of a cent. On thin-margin businesses that's the difference
  between viable and not.
- **Private by default.** Your sales aren't public. Your customers'
  payments aren't public. Your competitors can't shadow your revenue.

You can convert ZEC to fiat any time you want via an exchange. Holding
ZEC on the balance sheet is a *choice*, not a requirement.

## FAQ

### Do I have to hold ZEC?

No. You can swap to fiat or stablecoin immediately via an exchange. We
recommend running a daily or weekly swap rather than holding, unless
you've decided to hold deliberately.

### What about Malaysian tax?

You owe what you'd owe on a sale in any other currency. Your invoices
and memo-tagged transaction records are the basis for your books. We
can help set up a viewing-key feed for your bookkeeper — see
[Selective Disclosure](/using/selective-disclosure/).

### What if a customer pays the wrong amount?

The transaction is final, but you can refund partially or fully by
sending ZEC back. Same as cash, with a paper trail you control.

### What if I lose my phone?

You restore from your seed phrase on a new phone. The Zashi wallet
behind ZGo is self-custody — your funds aren't tied to the phone, just
to the seed.
