## Site roadmap

### Now

- Core curriculum (Modules 1 to 5) live.
- ZNS pages: landing, 60-second start, claim, merchants, team.
- Brand system: yellow/black + red/white, editorial layout.

### Next

- Real team photos on the [Team page](/team/).
- Live event dates and venues wired into [/ns](/ns/).
- Hosted claim form endpoint (replacing the email fallback).
- `adam@zcash.school` inbox / scheduling channel for merchant setup.

### Later

- Per-lesson "test yourself" checks.
- Printable one-pagers for events (banner-matched).
- Localised content for the NS cohort's primary languages.
