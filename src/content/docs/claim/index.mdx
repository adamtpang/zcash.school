## FAQ

### What's a shielded address?

A shielded Zcash address hides the recipient and amount of a payment
from anyone watching the chain. The Unified Address format (starting
with `u1`) bundles multiple shielded receivers so the sender's wallet
picks the most private one both sides support.

If you want the full picture: [/protocol/addresses](/protocol/addresses/).

### Where do I find my Unified Address?

In **Zashi/Zodl**: tap **Receive**. The string at the top, starting
with `u1`, is your UA. Copy it.

In **Ywallet** or **Nighthawk**: tap **Receive** and select the
Unified Address option if multiple are shown.

If you don't have a wallet yet: [/start](/start/), 60 seconds.

### How long does the airdrop take?

We batch claims daily. **Most claims arrive within a few hours.** The
24-hour window is the worst case (e.g. if you submit late at night
local time). If you're at an event in person, we may send on the spot.

### Is this taxable?

Probably. We're not your accountant. In most jurisdictions, an airdrop
counts as ordinary income at fair-market value on receipt. Keep a
record. Five dollars isn't going to change your life either way, but
the principle matters.

### Can I claim more than once?

One claim per person per ZNS program. Multiple submissions from the
same address won't increase your payout, they'll just slow our review.
