## What this is

**Zcash Network School (ZNS)** is a 90-day Zcash Foundation–funded program
running at [Network School](https://ns.com) in Malaysia, May through July
2026. The deliverables: 6 educational events, 12 office hours, 100 wallet
activations, 4 merchant POS installs, and 3 monthly forum reports back to
the foundation.

The people running it: **Andre Froes** (program lead), **James Joseph**
(direction, builder of [zcash.me](https://zcash.me) and
[zcashnames.com](https://zcashnames.com)), **Keiara Desouza** and **Souvik
Mishra** (execution), and **Adam** (events, vendor onboarding, this site).
We are independent of the Electric Coin Company and the Zcash Foundation;
the foundation funds the work, the work is ours.

What's actually happening on campus: short evening sessions teaching the
zero-knowledge thesis from scratch, weekly office hours for hands-on
debugging, and merchant onboarding for the cafés, food vendors, and
service businesses already operating at NS. **If you scanned a banner QR
to get here, you're in the right place.**

## What ZNS is *not*

A few honest clarifications, because the crypto world deserves more of these:

- We don't take a cut of anything. The $5 airdrops are funded by the
  grant. ZGo and CipherPay setup help is free.
- We aren't pumping ZEC. If you stop by an event and want to argue
  monetary policy, we'll point you at [forum.zcashcommunity.com](https://forum.zcashcommunity.com/).
- We aren't your wallet. Self-custody, write down your seed, etc. We
  teach the principles; you hold the keys.
