## Sister projects

zcash.school is not a standalone site. It's part of a small family of
ZNS-aligned projects, built by overlapping people, pointing at each
other on purpose. If you're learning Zcash through one, the others are
the next step.

How the three fit together:

- **zcash.me** — get a wallet, get moving. The shallow end.
- **zcash.school** (here) — understand *why* it works: privacy, ZK
  proofs, the protocol, the economics. The curriculum.
- **zcashnames.com** — make Zcash usable day-to-day with names instead
  of address strings. The usability layer.
