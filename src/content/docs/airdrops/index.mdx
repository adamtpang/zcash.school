## FAQ

### What is the "shielded newsletter"?

A short, occasional email from the Zcash Network School team about
what we shipped, what we learned, and what's next. We're still
deciding the exact mechanics; the email collected here is for that.

### Where do I find my Unified Address?

In **[Zodl](/start/)** (formerly Zashi): tap **Receive**. The string
at the top, starting with `u1`, is your UA. Copy that.

In **Ywallet** or **Nighthawk**: tap **Receive** and pick the Unified
Address option if multiple are shown.

### How long does the airdrop take?

We process claims daily. Most arrive within a few hours. The 24-hour
window is the worst case (e.g. late-night submission). If you're at
an event in person, we may send on the spot.

### Is this taxable?

Probably, in most jurisdictions. We're not your accountant. An airdrop
usually counts as ordinary income at fair-market value on receipt.
Keep a record.

### Where does my address actually go?

Straight into the **Airdrops Team** spreadsheet via a Google Apps
Script. No third-party CRM, no analytics provider that touches the
data, no Resend, no leakage. The team works directly in Sheets.
