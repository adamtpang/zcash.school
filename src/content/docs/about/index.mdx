> *Tuition free, knowledge zero.*

**zcash.school** is a free, opinionated course in Zcash: privacy, zero
knowledge proofs, and how shielded money actually works. It assumes
nothing and links to primary sources wherever they exist.

## Why this exists

Most "learn crypto" material is either a token shill or a whitepaper
firehose. Zcash deserves the thing in between: a calm, accurate,
end-to-end explanation a curious person can finish in an afternoon and
come out actually understanding zero-knowledge money.

This site is the curriculum layer of a small family of projects:

- **[zcash.me](https://zcash.me)**: get a wallet, get moving. The
  shallow end.
- **zcash.school** (here): understand why it works. The course.
- **[zcashnames.com](https://zcashnames.com)**: human-readable names
  instead of raw `u1...` strings. The usability layer.

## Who builds it

zcash.school is one deliverable of the **Zcash Network School (ZNS)**,
a 90-day program supported by a Zcash Foundation community grant,
running at [Network School](https://ns.com) in Malaysia.

Full team and sister-project detail lives on the
**[Team page](/team/)**. The short version: Andre Froes leads the
program, James Joseph drives direction and the sister sites, Keiara
Desouza and Souvik Mishra execute, and Adam runs events, vendor
onboarding, and this site.

## What it is, and is not

- It **is** a course, a reference, and a set of practical guides
  (wallets, first shielded transaction, accepting Zcash as a merchant).
- It **is** independent. The Zcash Foundation funds the ZNS program;
  the editorial work and opinions here are ours.
- It is **not** affiliated with the Electric Coin Company, the Zcash
  Foundation, or ZODL. We link to them; we do not speak for them.
- It is **not** a wallet, an exchange, a price page, or investment
  advice.
