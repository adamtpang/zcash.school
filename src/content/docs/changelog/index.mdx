> *Including the corrections. Especially the corrections.*

Newest first. Dates are when it went live on
[zcash.school](https://zcash.school).

## 2026-05-16

- **Editorial redesign.** Red/white zebra-print background with white
  content panels, magazine-style. Identity locked to Zcash yellow +
  black with a red/white spin.
- **Logo removed.** The old "ZS" monogram is gone; wordmark-only with a
  small yellow+red identity mark.
- **Top navbar added.** About, Team, Roadmap, Changelog, Network
  School, plus Discord and X.
- **Every em dash removed** sitewide (337 of them) for a cleaner
  editorial voice.
- **Zashi to Zodl correction.** Reflected the January 2026 ECC team
  spinout to ZODL and the wallet rename across the wallet, ecosystem,
  and resources pages. See [Wallets](/using/wallets/).
- New pages: [About](/about/), [Roadmap](/roadmap/),
  [Changelog](/changelog/), [Team](/team/).

## 2026-05-11

- **Q1 2026 ecosystem update.** Documented the restructure toward five
  independent orgs and treasury developments in
  [Ecosystem](/ecosystem/the-three-orgs/).
- Tone pass across the curriculum; sister-project framing for
  [zcash.me](https://zcash.me) and [zcashnames.com](https://zcashnames.com).
- Added [Project Tachyon](/protocol/project-tachyon/) to the protocol
  module.

## 2026-05-10

- **Launch.** zcash.school live on Vercel with the custom domain.
- Full curriculum: Why Privacy, Zero-Knowledge Proofs, How Zcash Works,
  Using Zcash, Ecosystem & Economics, plus Resources.
- ZNS program pages: [/ns](/ns/), [/start](/start/), [/claim](/claim/),
  [/merchants](/merchants/), with a printable banner QR.
