> *Be specific or be blunt; either helps.*

This site, the events, the airdrops, the curriculum — all of it gets
better when you say what's wrong with it.

The only required field is the feedback itself. Name, email, and which
page or topic are all optional. We will not add you to any list. There
is no newsletter signup hidden in here; that lives on
**[/airdrops](/airdrops/)** and only if you choose it.

<FeedbackForm />

<Aside type="note" title="Where this goes">
Straight into the **Zcash Network School team** shared Google Sheet
via Apps Script. No third-party form provider, no Resend, no
analytics tool that touches the content. The team reads it directly.
</Aside>

## Other ways to reach us

- **Public, on the forum:**
  [forum.zcashcommunity.com/t/zcash-network-school/55269](https://forum.zcashcommunity.com/t/zcash-network-school/55269).
  Best for substantive discussion or anything you would want other
  attendees and the Zcash Foundation to see.
- **In person:** drop into the next [office hours](/ns/#schedule)
  with whatever is on your mind. The session is built around this.
- **An issue on the repo:**
  [github.com/adamtpang/zcash.school](https://github.com/adamtpang/zcash.school/issues)
  if it is a concrete bug or content fix on the site.

<Aside type="tip" title="No wrong feedback">
"This page is too long." "The QR card has a typo." "The merchant
section assumes I already get crypto." "The blog post was great."
"You misspelled my name in the team page." All useful. Send it.
</Aside>
