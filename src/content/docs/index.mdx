## What you'll learn

This is a free, opinionated, end-to-end course in **Zcash** — the privacy-preserving
cryptocurrency that uses zero-knowledge proofs to keep transaction amounts and
parties private by default. We assume nothing. By the end you'll understand:

- Why financial privacy matters and what Bitcoin gave up
- How zero-knowledge proofs work, including zk-SNARKs and the trustless **Halo 2** system
- How Zcash structures addresses, pools, notes, and nullifiers
- How to actually use Zcash — wallets, shielded transactions, and selective disclosure
- The orgs, governance process, and economics of the network

## Who this is for

Curious newcomers, Bitcoin holders looking for privacy, developers who want a
solid mental model before reading the spec, and journalists or policy folks who
need accurate primary-source material on how shielded money works.
